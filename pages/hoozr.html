The Vision

Good Vibes. Good Causes.

Hoozr is part volunteer platform, part social network — built entirely around doing good in your local community.

Shelter Matching

Volunteers find shelters near them that need help, filter by animal type, time commitment, and skills.

Shift Scheduling

Shelters post volunteer slots; colanteers sign up, get reminders, and log their hours — all in one place.

Community Feed

Share your shelter experiences, post adoption success stories, and inspire others to get involved.

Outreach Tools

Built-in tools for shelters to run fundraisers, adoption events, and community drives.

Who It's For

Colanteers & Shelters Alike

Colanteers

Find nearby shelters, track volunteer hours, connect with other animal lovers, celebrate adoptions, and grow your impact.

Shelters

Post volunteer needs, manage schedules, run campaigns, share updates, and build a loyal community of supporters.
